# SVGimbal 

SVG to 3D spinning logo. Drop in an SVG, get a 3D render. Chrome, gold and gradient finishes, spin, and export to GIF, PNG, GLB, OBJ or STL.

Drop in an SVG, get a 3D version of it. Spin your logo and export the loop as a GIF. Runs entirely in the browser.


## Using it

Drag an SVG onto the canvas or hit the button. Then push depth, bevel and materials around until it looks right. Drag the model to move it, drag the background to orbit, scroll to zoom.

Export as PNG, GLB, OBJ, STL, or a looping GIF of it spinning.

## Spinning logo export

Flick on the spin toggle, set the speed, pick GIF in the export dropdown. You get a looping GIF of your logo turning a full 360, sized 360, 480 or 640 wide.

The loop is seamless because it captures exactly one revolution across 36 frames and works the frame delay back from your spin speed. Slower spin, longer GIF, same smooth loop.

Two buttons keep you out of trouble while you fiddle: **Reset angles** squares the logo back up, **Return camera** puts the view back where it started.

## Materials

Matte, plastic, metal, chrome, gold, copper, gradient, glass. The metals set their own colour. The rest use whatever colours are in your SVG unless you override them.
